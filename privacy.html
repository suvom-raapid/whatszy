Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WhatsFormat. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

WhatsFormat is a client-side application that runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal text, messages, or formatting data to our servers.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Your formatted text or messages
  • Personal identification information
  • WhatsApp account details
  • Contact information unless you voluntarily provide it

What We MAY Collect:

  • Analytics Data: We may use Google Analytics or similar services to understand how users interact with our website (page views, session duration, device type, geographic location)
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device for analytics and advertising purposes
  • Advertising Data: Google AdSense may collect data for personalized advertising

3. How We Use Your Information

Any data we collect through analytics is used to:

  • Improve user experience and website performance
  • Understand user behavior and preferences
  • Optimize content and features
  • Display relevant advertisements

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and understanding user behavior.
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google may collect technical information about your device.

5. Data Security

Since all text processing happens locally in your browser, your data never leaves your device. We do not store any of your formatted text or personal messages on our servers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Use browser extensions to block ads or analytics
  • Request information about data collected (if any)

7. Cookies Policy

We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics (performance and analytics cookies)
  • Google AdSense (advertising cookies)

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality and ad personalization.

8. Children's Privacy

WhatsFormat does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. International Users

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and may be accessed from anywhere in the world. By using WhatsFormat, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside of your country of residence.
