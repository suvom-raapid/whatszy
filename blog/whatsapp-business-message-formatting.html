How to Format WhatsApp Messages for Business

Business 5 min read February 25, 2026

WhatsApp is not just for personal chats anymore. With over 2 billion users worldwide, it has become a critical channel for business communication — from customer support and appointment reminders to product announcements and marketing campaigns. But a wall of plain text does not exactly scream "professional." Here is how to format your business messages so they are clear, scannable, and effective.

Why Formatting Matters in Business

Your customers skim. That is not a criticism — it is a fact of modern communication. People glance at messages on the go, between tasks, during commutes. If your important information is buried in a dense paragraph, it will get missed.

Well-formatted messages deliver several advantages for business communication:

  • Readability: Structured text is easier to parse at a glance. Customers find what they need without re-reading.
  • Professionalism: Clean formatting signals that you care about the details, which builds trust in your brand.
  • Higher response rates: Messages with clear calls-to-action and highlighted key information get more replies.
  • Reduced follow-ups: When customers can quickly find order numbers, dates, or instructions, they ask fewer repeat questions.

The good news is that WhatsApp supports several formatting options natively. If you are new to these basics, our guide on bold, italic, and strikethrough formatting covers the fundamentals.

Bold for Key Information

Bold is your most powerful tool for business messages. Wrap any text in asterisks (*like this*) to make it stand out. Use it for product names, prices, deadlines, and action items — anything the reader absolutely cannot miss.

Here is a well-formatted product inquiry response:

Hi Sarah! Thanks for your interest. *Product:* Premium Wireless Earbuds *Price:* $79.99 (10% off this week!) *Availability:* In stock — ships within 24 hours *Colors:* Midnight Black, Cloud White, Ocean Blue Would you like to place an order? Just reply with your preferred color.

Notice how every key detail is bolded and placed on its own line. The customer can scan this in seconds and immediately find what they need.

Bullet Lists for Clarity

WhatsApp does not have a built-in bullet feature, but you can create clean lists using the bullet character followed by a space. This format works perfectly for service menus, feature lists, and pricing tiers.

*Our Spa Packages:* • *Classic Facial* — 60 min — $85 • *Deep Tissue Massage* — 90 min — $120 • *Couples Package* — 120 min — $199 • *VIP Day Pass* — Full day — $299 All packages include complimentary refreshments. Reply to book your session!

Lists make complex information digestible. Instead of cramming five offerings into a paragraph, each one gets its own line with consistent formatting. Your customers will appreciate it.

Monospace for Codes & References

When sharing order numbers, tracking codes, promo codes, or reference IDs, wrap them in three backticks (```like this```) to render them in monospace font. This visually separates codes from regular text, making them easy to find and copy. For more details on this technique, see our guide to monospace and code blocks.

Your order has been confirmed! *Order ID:* ```ORD-2026-48291``` *Tracking:* ```TRK9847562301``` *Estimated Delivery:* March 2, 2026 Use promo code ```WELCOME15``` for 15% off your next purchase.

The monospace formatting makes codes instantly identifiable. Customers can spot them at a glance without scanning through surrounding text.

Template Messages

Having pre-formatted templates saves time and ensures consistency across your team. Here are three essential templates every business should have ready.

Order Confirmation:

*Order Confirmed!* ✅ Hi [Name], thank you for your purchase! *Order:* ```[Order ID]``` *Items:* [Product list] *Total:* *$[Amount]* *Delivery:* [Date range] Questions? Reply to this message anytime. Thank you for choosing [Business Name]!

Appointment Reminder:

*Appointment Reminder* 📅 Hi [Name], this is a reminder for your upcoming appointment: *Date:* [Day, Month Date, Year] *Time:* [Time] *Location:* [Address] *Service:* [Service name] Need to reschedule? Please reply at least 24 hours in advance. See you soon!

Product Announcement:

*New Arrival Alert!* 🚀 Introducing the *[Product Name]* — [one-line description]. • [Feature 1] • [Feature 2] • [Feature 3] *Launch Price:* $[Price] (regular $[Higher Price]) *Available:* [Date] Reply *ORDER* to reserve yours today!

Save these templates in your phone's notes app or WhatsApp Business quick replies. Customize the bracketed fields for each customer, and you will have polished messages ready to send in seconds.

Tips for Professional Messaging

Formatting is just one piece of the puzzle. These broader tips will help you communicate more effectively on WhatsApp as a business.

  • Keep it concise. Respect your customer's time. If a message needs to be long, break it into sections with bold headings. Be aware of WhatsApp's character limits as well.
  • Use line breaks generously. White space is your friend. Double-tap Enter between sections to create visual breathing room.
  • Don't overformat. If everything is bold, nothing is bold. Reserve formatting for truly important elements.
  • Include a clear CTA. Every business message should tell the customer what to do next — reply, click, confirm, or order.
  • Be consistent with brand voice. Whether your tone is casual or formal, keep it uniform across all messages. Consistency builds recognition.
  • Add relevant emojis sparingly. A single well-placed emoji can add warmth without looking unprofessional. Check out our guide to emoji combinations for tasteful options.

The difference between an amateur business message and a professional one often comes down to these small details. A few minutes spent formatting can dramatically improve how customers perceive your brand.
