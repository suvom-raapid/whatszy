What is WhatsFormat?

WhatsFormat is a free online toolkit with 12 tools for formatting and styling your WhatsApp messages. Make text bold, italic, or strikethrough, generate fancy Unicode fonts, send invisible blank messages, repeat text, flip words upside down, pick emojis, create click-to-chat links, convert text case, generate tiny superscript text, create Zalgo glitch effects, or turn words into emoji letter blocks — everything right here in your browser.

All processing happens locally on your device. We never see, store, or transmit your messages. No downloads, no sign-ups — just open the page and start formatting. WhatsFormat works on phones, tablets, and desktops.
