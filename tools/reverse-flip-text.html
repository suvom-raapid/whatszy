Reverse & Flip Text Generator

Turn your text backwards or flip it completely upside down with this free online tool. The Reverse effect mirrors your text character by character, so 'Hello' becomes 'olleH'. The Upside Down effect goes further — it replaces each letter with its Unicode upside-down equivalent and reverses the order, creating text that looks like it was physically flipped. Both effects produce eye-catching results in WhatsApp chats, Instagram bios, and social media posts. Simply type your text and both outputs update in real-time. Click on either result to copy it instantly. These Unicode characters work on all platforms and devices without any special apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Reversed text simply mirrors the character order ('Hello' becomes 'olleH'). Upside down replaces each character with its Unicode upside-down equivalent and reverses the order, creating text that looks physically flipped.

Most modern devices support Unicode upside-down characters. Very old phones might show some characters as squares. The reversed effect works universally since it uses regular characters.

Emojis cannot be flipped because there are no upside-down Unicode equivalents for emoji characters. Letters, numbers, and basic punctuation can be flipped.

Flipped and reversed text works on WhatsApp, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, TikTok, Discord, and any platform that accepts text input.
