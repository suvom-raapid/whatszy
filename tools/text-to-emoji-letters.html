Text to Emoji Letters

Turn your words into large, eye-catching emoji letter blocks. This tool converts each letter (A-Z) into its corresponding Regional Indicator Symbol — the same Unicode characters used for flag emojis. Numbers (0-9) become keycap emojis like 1️⃣ 2️⃣ 3️⃣. The result is text that stands out dramatically in WhatsApp chats and group messages. It is perfect for announcements, headers, greetings, and any message that needs to grab attention. Keep your text short for best results — one to three words work best since each letter becomes a full-sized emoji block. Spaces are preserved between words. The emoji letters work on all platforms including WhatsApp, Instagram, and Twitter.
