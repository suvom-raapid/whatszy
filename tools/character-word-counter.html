Character & Word Counter

Need to know exactly how long your text is? This Character & Word Counter analyzes your text in real-time, showing four key metrics: character count, word count, line count, and emoji count. It is essential for staying within WhatsApp's limits — approximately 65,000 characters per message and 700 characters for status updates. Paste any text to instantly see a detailed breakdown. The counter updates as you type, making it easy to trim content to fit specific limits. Beyond WhatsApp, it is useful for Twitter posts, SMS messages, meta descriptions, and any situation where character count matters. All counting happens locally in your browser.
