Emoji Picker for WhatsApp

Finding the perfect emoji shouldn't require scrolling through your phone's tiny emoji keyboard. This Emoji Picker gives you quick access to over 200 popular emojis organized into 8 clear categories: Smileys, Hands, Hearts, Animals, Food, Travel, Symbols, and Flags. Click on any emoji to add it to your collection, build creative emoji combinations, then copy everything at once. It's especially useful on desktop where emoji keyboards can be clunky, or when you want to build emoji sequences for WhatsApp messages, Instagram captions, or social media posts. All emojis are standard Unicode and display correctly on every modern device.

How to Use

  1. Browse emoji categories by clicking the category buttons (Smileys, Hands, Hearts, etc.)
  2. Click on any emoji to add it to your collection at the bottom
  3. Build emoji combinations by clicking multiple emojis in sequence
  4. Click "Copy Emojis" to copy your entire collection
  5. Paste into WhatsApp or any other app
